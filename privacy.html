Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026
SubWise collects zero user data.

Data storage

All subscription data you enter is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your private iCloud container managed entirely by Apple. SubWise has no server and cannot access your iCloud data.

Data we do not collect

SubWise does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial information (bank details, credit card numbers)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Analytics or usage tracking data
  • Location data
  • Cookies or web tracking

Third-party services

SubWise uses the frankfurter.app API to fetch currency exchange rates. These requests are anonymous GET requests and contain no user data, device identifiers, or tracking information.

SubWise uses Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple under Apple's privacy policy. SubWise does not receive or store your payment details.

iCloud sync

If you enable iCloud sync in Settings, your subscription data syncs across your Apple devices using Apple's CloudKit framework. This data is stored in your private iCloud container and is not accessible to SubWise or any third party. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app's Settings screen. Disabling sync does not delete your local data.

Camera access

SubWise Pro includes an optional receipt scanning feature that uses your device's camera. Camera access is only requested when you tap "Scan receipt." All text recognition happens on-device using Apple's Vision framework — no images are uploaded or transmitted anywhere. Camera access can be revoked at any time in your device's Settings.

Notifications

SubWise uses local notifications to remind you about upcoming subscription renewals and trial expirations. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external push notification server. No notification data leaves your device.

Children's privacy

SubWise does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. Since SubWise collects no data from any user, there is no risk of children's data being collected.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy if the app's functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Since SubWise collects no data, meaningful changes to this policy are unlikely.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at businessforzeeshan@gmail.com.
