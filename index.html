The backstory

I sat down and added up every recurring charge hitting my account.

Netflix. Spotify. ChatGPT. iCloud. Apple One. A gym membership I'd forgotten about. A VPN. Two SaaS tools I used once. A cloud service from 2021.

I genuinely thought I was spending around $80/month. The real number was over $200.

I'm an iOS developer — 8 years professionally, government clients, 600K+ users, apps that have been in production for 5 years. SubWise is the first app I shipped under my own name.

Every other app in this space wants your bank login. I refused. Your subscription data is personal. It should stay on your device. No account, no login, no data collected. Not because it's a feature — because it's the right way to build it.

The app is native Swift, built from scratch. Not a template. Not a no-code tool. Not vibe-coded. Every screen, every animation, every line of code — written deliberately, tested on real devices, and shipped with intent.

The Pro version is $6.99. Once. Forever. Making a subscription tracker that charges a monthly fee felt deeply wrong.

What SubWise does
168 services
Type "Net" and SubWise auto-fills Netflix with the right price, category, and logo. Works for Spotify, ChatGPT, iCloud, Disney+, and 164 more services.
The real total
Dashboard shows your actual monthly and yearly spend with a category breakdown. Entertainment, AI tools, cloud — see exactly where the money goes.
Calendar view
Every upcoming renewal projected on a calendar with color-coded dots. You know what's coming before it hits your account.
Smart reminders
Get notified 1, 3, or 7 days before any renewal. Free trial alerts are marked urgent so you cancel before you're charged.
50+ currencies
USD, AED, EUR, GBP, SAR — track subscriptions in any currency and SubWise converts everything to your home currency automatically.
Price increase alerts
SubWise detects when a service raises its price and shows you the annual impact. Netflix went up $7.50/month? That's $90/year extra.
Widgets
See your monthly spend on your home screen without opening the app. Trial countdowns on the Dynamic Island. Four widget sizes.
AI receipt scanner
Point your camera at a receipt or billing email. SubWise extracts the service, amount, and date. All on-device — nothing leaves your phone.
