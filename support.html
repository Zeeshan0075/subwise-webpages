Support

Find answers to common questions below, or contact us directly.

How do I add a subscription?

Tap the green "+" button on any screen. Start typing the service name — SubWise will suggest from 168+ popular services with auto-filled pricing and category. Adjust any details, then tap Save.

How do I edit or delete a subscription?

In the Subscriptions tab, swipe left on any subscription to see Archive and Delete options. Swipe right to Edit. You can also tap a subscription to view its full details, then tap Edit or Delete from there.

How do I change my currency?

Go to Settings → Home currency → Select your preferred currency from 50+ options. All totals across the app will automatically convert to your new home currency.

What is SubWise Pro?

SubWise Pro is a one-time purchase of $6.99 that unlocks unlimited subscriptions, AI receipt scanning, premium widgets, spending trends and insights, CSV/PDF export, custom categories, and alternate app icons. It is not a subscription — you pay once and keep it forever.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → scroll down to Legal → Tap "Restore purchases." If you previously purchased SubWise Pro on any device with the same Apple ID, it will be restored automatically at no additional charge.

Is my data private?

Yes — completely. SubWise stores all data locally on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your data. If you enable iCloud sync, your data syncs through Apple's private CloudKit container, which only you can access. We earn the App Store's "Data Not Collected" privacy label because we genuinely collect nothing.

How does the receipt scanner work?

The AI scanner uses Apple's Vision framework to read text from a receipt or billing email screenshot. It extracts the service name, amount, currency, and date, then lets you review and confirm before saving. All processing happens on your device — no images are uploaded anywhere. This feature requires SubWise Pro.

How do I add widgets to my home screen?

Long-press on your iPhone home screen → Tap the "+" button in the top-left → Search for "SubWise" → Choose a widget size (small, medium, or large) → Tap "Add Widget." The small widget is free; medium, large, and lock screen widgets require SubWise Pro.

Does SubWise work offline?

Yes. All core features work without an internet connection. The only feature that requires connectivity is currency exchange rate updates, which refresh every 7 days. If you're offline, SubWise uses the last cached exchange rates.

Can I export my subscription data?

Yes — with SubWise Pro, go to Settings → Export data → Choose CSV or PDF format. The export includes all your subscription details and can be shared via email, AirDrop, or any other sharing method. This feature requires SubWise Pro.
